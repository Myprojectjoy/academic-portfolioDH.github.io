Biography

I did my Ph.D. in Machine Learning Lab at Jeju National University, South Korea. After pursuing master’s in Computer Application, I worked as a software engineer at Atos Global IT Solutions and Services Pvt. Ltd. in India and Germany. I obtained bachelor’s degree in Computer Science Honors from Calcutta University, India. My specialization is in generating synthetic data using generative adversarial networks (GAN) and enhancing classification or prediction accuracy for disease diagnosis. During my Ph.D. studies, I have worked with biomedical signals, microbiomes, microscopic cell images, nucleic acid sequences and also with data from other domains.

I am currently an Assistant Professor at Montclair State University, School of Computing, College of Science and Mathematics, with a strong commitment to both research excellence and education, Dr. Hazra actively supervises under graduate students and contributes to the academic community through peer review, conference organization, and collaborative research initiatives.
